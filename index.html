TAMAGOTCHI GAME

A rendition of the classic Tamagotchi game that allows players to have 3 controls for their Tamagotchi character: feed, sleep and play. The Tamagotchi character has stat levels of sleepiness, boredom, hunger and age, which increase by different time intervals. The player must click on the proper controls to make sure the levels of each stat do not go past 20. The goal of the game is keep the Tamagotchi character alive; if the character dies, the player has the chance to start over. Check it out!
